Hi, my name is Alexander Haywood

Hi, my name is Alexander Haywood, and I am a master's student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Princeton. Before this, I received a Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering degree at Princeton, while concurrently pursuing minors in Physics and Applied Mathematics.

My research interests lie broadly in condensed matter theory. However, I am particularly interested in the study of novel models and phases. Before pursuing a PhD, I am pursuing research with Professor Biao Lian in the Princeton physics department.

This website is a compilation of my previous work and hobbies, all of which gave me the skills, experience, and context to pursue my current path in Physics.
